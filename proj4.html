Hermit Defense Game

This game was developed by me during my first semester at Boise State. The objective of the game is to survive against wolves that are attacking the hermit and his home. The hermit must use spears and tamed wolves to defend his home for as long as possible.

Controls
  • Top Left Wolf Button - Calls in wolf to fight enemy wolves.
  • Top Left Wall Button - Calls in wall to block incoming wolves.
  • Left Mouse Click - Throws spear at mouse position. If you hit an enemy they will be eliminated.
Caution: Game may have issues with screen size
