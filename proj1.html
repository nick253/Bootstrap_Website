Mar's Colony Game

This game was developed by me and two other student for a group project at Boise State University. The goal of the game is to survive against the incoming hoards of aliens coming to destroy your colony. You must work to keep your turrets operational before the aliens eliminate your defenses and reach your base.

Controls
  • W - fly upward
  • A - move left
  • D - move right
  • Spacebar - repair turrets
